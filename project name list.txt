5-“e-Asset Recovery Portal”

“e-Asset Recovery Portal” is a system that helps banks manage customers who fail to repay loans. 
The bank can seize their properties or assets and sell them through online or offline auctions to recover the money.
The portal tracks loan defaults, manages foreclosed properties, handles the auction process, and records payments in a digital and organized way.
